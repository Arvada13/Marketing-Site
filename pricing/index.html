FAQ

Can I try CloudCannon for free?

Yes! Signing up for CloudCannon starts a free 30 day trial with the enterprise plan features. During this trial period you can enter a credit card and select a plan if you'd like to continue using CloudCannon.

How am I billed when adding new users?

We'll make a one-time, prorated charge to your credit card to cover your new team member's account for the remainder of the current billing period.

For example, if your team is on the agency plan, it will cost $75 per user per month. You add a new team member 10 days into your billing period and there are 20 remaining days in the month. The per-day cost for each user is $75 ÷ 30, so we will make a one-time charge of $50 ($75 ÷ 30 days × 20 days) to your credit card on file.

CloudCannon almost fits my needs, do you offer X?

We listen closely to the feedback of our customers. If you need something more from CloudCannon, get in touch with support.

Where are CloudCannon sites hosted?

CloudCannon uses Amazon Web Services for all infrastructure. Sites are served from our custom File Server and automatically optimised. Optimisations include GZIP, serving assets from a CDN and minification.

CloudCannon also supports hosting sites on your own servers.

What happens after the 30 day trial?

If you haven't added a credit card and selected a plan, you'll be downgraded to our free plan. The free plan restricts access to your sites and removes all the shares you've made.

Do I have to pay for a user when I'm sharing to a client?

No, you can use Client Sharing which does not count as a user.

Can I switch plans?

Yes, you can change your plan any time in Account Settings.

How do I cancel my CloudCannon account?

You can either downgrade to the free plan or delete your account. You can find these options in Account Settings.
