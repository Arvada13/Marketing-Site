How does CloudCannon work?

1. Upload your website

You can upload your static or Jekyll website to CloudCannon in the browser or sync with Dropbox or GitHub.

2. Set Editable Regions

You have full control over the areas your non-technical team can update by adding class=”editable” to elements in the HTML.

3. Deploy

Your website can be automatically deployed to CloudCannon hosting or any other hosting service such as GitHub Pages.

4. Share with your team

Your team will love how easy it is to make changes on the website with our visual editor. On Jekyll websites they can also easily add and update blog posts, front matter and collections.

5. Synchronize

All changes made on CloudCannon are saved back to your original source code and pushed back to Dropbox or GitHub. Any changes made on Dropbox and GitHub are automatically pulled into CloudCannon.
