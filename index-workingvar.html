Working with CloudCannon

1. Create your site

Sign up for CloudCannon and create a new site. This will contain all the settings and files for your website.

2. Add your site files

Upload your static or Jekyll website files to CloudCannon in the browser. If you've got the files on GitHub, Dropbox or FTP, we can pull and keep them in sync for you.

3. Live!

See your site live on the web, CloudCannon will optimise and host your files. We also give you a free testing domain to start with. You'll be able to add your own domain later.

4. Set Editable Elements

Add class="editable" to elements in the HTML to control which elements clients can edit.

Clients make updates directly on the page, what they see is what they get. Your team and clients will love how intuitive and easy it is to update their website.

5. Sharing

Share your website with your client so they can edit the elements you've selected. Under the settings for your site, share to an email address or set a password for the edit URL.
