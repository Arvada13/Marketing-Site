George and I were building websites as freelancers while studying Computer Science in New Zealand. We loved creating fantastic experiences and pleasing our clients with unique designs, but a good portion of our time was spent setting up a CMS for each website. When we realised that it was the CMS dictating our workflow, we built a tool that puts creativity first.

Now our vision is much bigger.

We want to reduce the barrier to becoming a professional web designer by setting a new standard for usable, unobtrusive software in the web design industry.

Mike Neumegen - CEO / Cofounder
