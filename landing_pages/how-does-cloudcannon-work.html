1 Sync

Create a repository in GitHub or Bitbucket with your Jekyll or static site. Connect your repository to CloudCannon and it will download and keep future updates in sync for you.

2 Live!

Your site is live on the web. CloudCannon optimizes Javascript, CSS and other assets so your site loads insanely fast.

3 Share

Share your CloudCannon site with the Developers and Marketers on your team.

Marketers get a beautiful interface for updating Blogs, Collections and Front Matter. To make content on a page editable for Marketers add class="editable" to elements in the HTML.

4 Work your way

You can continue working in your local environment with your favourite tools. When you want to sync changes to CloudCannon just push to your repository.

Changes made in CloudCannon also get pushed back to the repository so you can always pull in an up-to-date version of the site.

1 Preview

Once your Developer shares the site with you, login to CloudCannon get a live preview of the site.

2 Update Content

Yellow boxes indicate the content you can update. Click on one and update content directly on the page. What you see is exactly what it will look like on the live site.

3 Manage the Blog

If your site has a blog or collections you can manage all posts easily and visually. No coding required.

4 Live

Saved changes are instantly pushed to the live website. Have control over all the content on your site.
